1. NANOMATERIALS

Definition

Nanomaterials are materials having at least one dimension in the range of 1–100 nm.

Characteristics

• Large surface area
• High strength
• Improved electrical properties
• Quantum effects
• High chemical reactivity


---

2. GRAPHENE

Definition

Graphene is a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in hexagonal structure.

Thickness = 0.34 nm

Bond length = 0.142 nm

Hybridization = SP²


---

Properties

• High electrical conductivity
• High thermal conductivity
• High strength
• Flexible
• Transparent
• Large surface area


---

Applications

• Transistors
• Sensors
• Batteries
• Solar cells
• LEDs
• Biomedical devices


---

Structure

SP² hybridized carbon atoms arranged in hexagonal lattice

π electrons responsible for conductivity

---

SOLVED PROBLEMS

Problem 1

Convert 50 nm to meter

Solution

1 nm = 10⁻⁹ m

50 nm = 50 × 10⁻⁹ m

= 5 × 10⁻⁸ m


---

Problem 2

Calculate strain if stress = 200 MPa
Young's modulus = 100 GPa

Solution

E = Stress / Strain

Strain = Stress / E

= 200 ×10⁶ / 100 ×10⁹

= 2 ×10⁻³
